Rocket Boost

A showcase of my game development project

Overview

Rocket Boost is an exciting game development project where I focused on creating a futuristic environment and interactive gameplay mechanics. The game involves controlling a rocket through challenging levels in a visually stunning sci-fi world.

Case Study

Rocket Boost was developed to push the boundaries of what I could achieve in game design. It combines engaging mechanics, smooth animations, and an immersive world. Here’s what I focused on:

  • Level Design: Creating a balance between challenge and fun.
  • Physics: Implementing realistic physics for the rocket movement.
  • Visual Aesthetics: Designing a futuristic world with attention to detail.

What I Learned

This project helped me grow as a game developer. Here are some key takeaways:

  • Improved skills in Unity game development, including physics and animations.
  • Learned to optimize assets to ensure smooth performance on different devices.
  • Enhanced my ability to balance difficulty and playability in game design.
  • Gained insights into player psychology and how to design for engagement.
