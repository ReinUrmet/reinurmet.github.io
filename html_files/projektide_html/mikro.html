Mikro Game

A Pygame project where players control Mikro And the enemies come and attack you in rounds

Overview

I Created this game with a friend from school form my programming 1 class. In this came you play as a character called Micro and have to survitve ten rounds of ever increacing enemies.

What I Learned

Through this project, I enhanced my skills in several areas:

  • Attacking: Implementing the brush and making it so that it cuts the enemies in half depending on the direction it's coming from.
  • Enemy AI: Designing basic enemy behavior and making them move well.
  • Graphics and Animations: Creating smooth character animations and a visually appealing environment.
  • Event Handling: Managing user inputs for movement and interaction.
